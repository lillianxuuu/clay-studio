Clay Studio Challenge: Tall vase
Paint patterns

First piece

  1. Drag across the spinning clay. Horizontal movement widens or narrows the active ring.
  2. Drag up to pull the clay taller. Drag down to compress it.
  3. Use Smooth to soften hard transitions, then dry and fire the pot.
  4. Paint with colors and decorative brushes, choose a glaze, then display it.
